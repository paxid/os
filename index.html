Getting Started

Open the dock icons to launch Ubuntu-inspired applications.

  • Terminal supports commands like help, ls, cd, apt, and open <file>.
  • Files and Terminal share the same virtual filesystem; double-click text files to edit them.
  • Settings lets you change the wallpaper, accent colours, and clock format.
